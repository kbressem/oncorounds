About

OncoRounds tests clinical reasoning through interactive case solving:

  • 20 expert-created oncology cases
  • 3 clinical rounds with incremental information
  • Models request tests, then diagnose and plan treatment
  • Scored on diagnosis, differentials, and treatment plan
